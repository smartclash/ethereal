About KCG College

KCG College of Technology was founded in 1998 to fulfill the Founder-Chairman, Dr. KCG Verghese's vision of "To Make Every Man a Success and No Man a Failure".

This young and vibrant institution focuses on quality engineering education and a spirit of innovation along with holistic personality development.
